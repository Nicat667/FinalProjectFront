About this hotel

Set in Hanoi and with Vietnam Museum of Ethnology reachable within 1.7 km, VNAHOMES APARTHOTEL offers express check-in and check-out, non-smoking rooms, a shared lounge, free WiFi throughout the property and a garden. Boasting family rooms, this property also provides guests with a terrace. The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk and luggage storage space for guests.
At the hotel, the rooms are equipped with a desk, a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, bed linen and towels. All rooms include a kettle, while some rooms also feature a balcony and others also feature city views. All guest rooms at VNAHOMES APARTHOTEL feature air conditioning and a wardrobe.
Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Hanoi, like cycling.
West Lake is 2.5 km from VNAHOMES APARTHOTEL, while Quan Thanh Temple is 2.9 km from the property. The nearest airport is Noi Bai International, 18 km from the hotel, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 10 for a two-person trip.
